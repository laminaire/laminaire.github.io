Intake

A window-mounted airflow
regulator that intelligently
draws filtered air indoors.

Sensors detect and judge how much air
should be drawn indoors to get the most
optimal air quality

Filters prevent micro-particulates from entering the room when air is drawn from outside

2-directional splash-proofing design that allows the unit to be mounted both horizontally for hung windows and vertically for sliding windows

Easily replaceable parts improve
the sustainability of the product
